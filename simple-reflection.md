# Simple Reflection for Testing

## What Happened

Yesterday, my partner and I had a disagreement about household chores. I asked them to help with the dishes after dinner, and they got defensive, saying I was "nagging" them.

I felt frustrated because I had been doing most of the housework lately. When I tried to explain this, they said I was being "dramatic" and walked away.

## How I Felt

I felt hurt and dismissed. It made me question whether they value my contributions to our home. I also felt angry that they wouldn't listen to my concerns.

## What I Need

I need them to acknowledge my feelings and help more around the house. I want to feel like we're partners, not like I'm doing everything alone.

## Questions

- How can I communicate my needs without them getting defensive?
- Is this a pattern in our relationship?
- What can I do differently next time?
